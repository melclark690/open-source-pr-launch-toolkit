## 📦 What's Inside the Toolkit

This toolkit provides a modular framework to successfully launch your open-source project. Explore the core components below:

* [📋 Launch Checklists](./LAUNCH_PLAN.md#%F0%9F%93%8B-launch-checklists-step-by-step-countdown-trackers) — Step-by-step countdown trackers for pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch milestones.
* [📧 Press & Communication Templates](./LAUNCH_PLAN.md#%F0%9F%93%A7-press--communication-templates) — Ready-to-use email pitches and hacker news copy blueprints.
* [🎤 Deck 1: Media Training Slides](./LAUNCH_PLAN.md#%F0%9F%8E%A4-deck-1-media-training-slides) — Complete slide guidelines to prepare core maintainers for public interviews.
* [🎤 Deck 2: Community Town Hall Slides](./LAUNCH_PLAN.md#%F0%9F%8E%A4-deck-2-community-town-hall-slides) — Slide frameworks designed for live streams and roadmap alignment.
* [📊 Launch Analytics Dashboard](./LAUNCH_PLAN.md#%F0%9F%93%8A-launch-analytics-dashboard) — Structured metrics to track repository views, clones, and community growth.
