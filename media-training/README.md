## 📦 What's Inside the Toolkit

This toolkit provides a modular framework to successfully launch your open-source project. Explore the core components below:

*   **📋 Launch Checklists (`/launch-checklists`)** — Step-by-step countdown trackers for pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch milestones.
*   **📧 Press & Communication Templates (`/templates`)** — Ready-to-use email pitches, hacker news copy, and structured press release blueprints.
*   **🎤 Deck 1: Media Training Slides (`/media-training`)** — A complete 7-slide educational deck designed to prepare core maintainers and executives for public interviews, live podcasts, and open-source ecosystem crisis triage.
