## 📦 What's Inside the Toolkit

This toolkit provides a modular framework to successfully launch your open-source project. Explore the core components below:

*   **📋 Launch Checklists** — [Step-by-step countdown trackers](./launch-checklists) for pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch milestones.
*   **📧 Press & Communication Templates** — [Ready-to-use email pitches and hacker news copy](./templates) blueprints.
*   **🎤 Deck 1: Media Training Slides** — A complete [7-slide educational deck](./media-training) designed to prepare core maintainers and executives for public interviews, live podcasts, and open-source ecosystem crisis triage.
